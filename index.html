Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection required.

How many free searches do I get?
Free plan includes 5 searches per day across up to 3 countries.

What does Pro include?
Unlimited searches, all 19 countries. One-time purchase, no subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app → tap Pro → Restore Purchases.
